# OhMyUniversity! - RAD: 1.2 Scope of the System

The scope of **OhMyUniversity** encompasses the development of a unified, multi-platform interface designed to centralize the management of a student's academic journey. Acting as an advanced **middleware layer**, the system orchestrates data from external university webservices, such as Cineca/Esse3 and GOMP, to provide real-time synchronization of academic records, including grades, weighted averages, and tuition fee statuses.

The application’s functional reach extends to:
* **Logistical Support**: Classroom bookings and transportation tracking via integrated maps.
* **Academic Resource Management**: Downloading lecture materials and monitoring attendance.
* **Strategic Planning**: Automating the verification of Master's degree requirements to eliminate manual checks of static PDFs.
* **Student-Company Connection**: Acting as a connection point between students and companies through
conventions, discounts, and cultural events.

The system's boundary is strictly limited to data organization and consultation; it **does not replace** official administrative systems for direct financial transactions or the formal recording of grades, which remain under the exclusive jurisdiction of the university’s institutional platforms.
