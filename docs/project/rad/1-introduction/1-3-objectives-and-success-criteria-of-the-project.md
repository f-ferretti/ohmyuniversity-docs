# OhMyUniversity! - RAD: 1.3 Objectives and Success Criteria

The primary objective of **OhMyUniversity** is to bridge the gap between students and the fragmented university ecosystem by providing a streamlined, high-performance digital hub. The project focuses on delivering a single access point that consolidates daily academic needs, such as career tracking, logistics, and educational resources. 

Key goals include:
* **Reducing Navigation Time**: Drastically cutting the time spent navigating multiple portals for schedules or grade averages.
* **Automating Monitoring**: Handling complex tracking for future academic requirements, such as Master's degree eligibility.
* **Facilitating Campus Life**: Integrating transportation tools and corporate partnerships directly into the student workflow.

To ensure these objectives are met, the project’s success is measured against strict, reachable technical parameters:

* **Data Integrity**: Calculations for grade averages and Master's requirements must maintain a **100% correspondence** with official Cineca data, ensuring zero error tolerance for academic records.
* **Operational Efficiency**: The system must allow an expert user to retrieve critical information (e.g., lesson schedules or grade averages) in **3 interactions (taps) or fewer** from the moment the app is launched.
* **Performance Reliability**: The backend must guarantee a **99.9% monthly uptime**, with data synchronization from Cineca APIs completing within **2.0 seconds**.
* **User Autonomy**: The interface must be intuitive enough for a new user to complete a classroom seat reservation in **under 60 seconds** without needing external documentation.
* **Integration Effectiveness**: The system must successfully redirect users to partner portals or generate valid, error-free discount codes to prove the viability of the corporate partnership module.
