# OhMyUniversity! - RAD: 1.1 Purpose of the System

The system was conceived to radically transform how students interact with their academic institutions. Currently, the student journey is often hindered by excessive bureaucracy and information scattered across obsolete web portals, static PDF files, and physical offices. 

**OhMyUniversity!** is designed as an active support tool that aims to:
* **Centralize Access**: Create a single point of entry for the entire academic life cycle.
* **Reduce Cognitive Load**: Automate the retrieval of information and the calculation of complex statistics (averages, graduation projections), allowing students to focus on their education.
* **Strategic Support**: Provide tools for long-term planning, such as monitoring requirements for future Master's degrees.
