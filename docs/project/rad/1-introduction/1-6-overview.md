# OhMyUniversity! - RAD: 1.6 Overview

This document is organized into four primary sections, providing a logical transition from the conceptual vision to the formal technical analysis:

* **Section 1: Introduction** (this section) provides the general context of the project, defining the system's purpose, scope, measurable success criteria, and a comprehensive list of references and technical definitions.
* **Section 2: Current System** describes the pre-existing landscape and legacy solutions. In this project, it focuses on the **Competitor Analysis**, highlighting the gaps in current university services that OhMyUniversity! aims to fill.
* **Section 3: Proposed System** represents the operational core of the document. It details the functional requirements (the "what"), the non-functional requirements (usability, performance, legal aspects, etc.), and the formal **System Models** (Scenarios, Use Cases, Object, and Dynamic models) including UI mock-ups.
* **Section 4: Glossary** provides a final dedicated reference to ensure linguistic precision and eliminate ambiguity regarding specific domain terms used throughout the analysis.

By following this structure, the RAD serves as the definitive agreement between stakeholders and the development team, evolving from initial requirements gathering to formal system analysis.
