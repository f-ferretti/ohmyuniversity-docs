# OhMyUniversity! - RAD: 1.5 References

The design, development, and requirement definitions for **OhMyUniversity** are based on the following international standards, regulatory frameworks, and technical documentation:

* **IEEE Standard 830-1998**: The international "Recommended Practice for Software Requirements Specifications," which serves as the foundational structure for this document.
* **GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679)**: The mandatory European framework for the protection and processing of sensitive personal data, such as student grades and biographical information.
* **AgID (Agenzia per l'Italia Digitale) Guidelines**: National standards for the accessibility and usability of university student applications. This includes protocols for integrating national digital identity systems such as SPID and CIE.
* **WCAG 2.1 (Level AA)**: International accessibility standards used to ensure the platform is usable by students with disabilities.
* **Cineca/Esse3 & GOMP Technical Documentation**: Official specifications for the integration of digital student office services and the retrieval of career data (grades, averages, and fees) via APIs.
* **Moodle LMS Documentation**: Technical references for integrating the learning management platform to allow direct access to course materials and lecture slides.
* **Google Maps Platform / OpenStreetMap Documentation**: Specifications for the integration of geolocation, campus mapping, and transportation tracking features.
* **University Teaching Regulations**: Institutional documents used to define the specific algorithms for calculating weighted averages, honor criteria, and Master's degree access requirements.
